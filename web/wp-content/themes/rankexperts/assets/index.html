Search Engine Optimization

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the practice of optimizing your website and online content to improve its visibility in search engine results. It involves various techniques and strategies to enhance your website's ranking in search engine pages like Google, Bing, and Yahoo.

SEO is crucial for businesses and website owners because it helps increase organic (non-paid) traffic to your site, which can lead to higher visibility, more visitors, and potential customers.

Effective SEO can also improve the user experience on your website and boost its credibility and trustworthiness.
